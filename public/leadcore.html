What lead sources does LeadCore connect to?

Web forms, CSV uploads, CRM exports, and direct API calls via webhook. If you can export your leads, LeadCore can score them. No native integration required.

How does it know what a "good" lead looks like?

You define your ICP criteria during setup — industry, company size, title, revenue range, tech stack. LeadCore applies those filters automatically to every new lead via a customisable GPT-4o scoring prompt.

We already use HubSpot / Salesforce / Pipedrive. Will this work?

Yes. LeadCore works alongside your existing CRM — it doesn't replace it. Think of it as the filter layer between your lead source and your reps. Hot leads are pushed into your CRM automatically.

How long does setup take?

Under 30 minutes for most teams. LeadCore is delivered as an import-ready n8n workflow with a step-by-step README and CREDENTIALS guide. No developer required.

Is this a subscription?

No. LeadCore is a one-time purchase — $79. You own the workflow. No monthly fees, no seat limits, no usage caps. You pay OpenAI and Apollo.io directly at roughly $0.05 per lead processed.
