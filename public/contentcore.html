FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before purchasing.

Will the articles rank on Google?

ContentCore is built to compete — it researches what's already ranking and structures the article accordingly. That said, SEO rankings depend on your domain authority, link profile, and content consistency over time. ContentCore handles output volume and structure; your broader SEO strategy handles the rest.

Does it publish directly or as a draft?

As a draft by default — the article is ready for your review before going live. You can change this to auto-publish in the workflow configuration. Most teams prefer the draft review step initially.

How do I control the writing tone and style?

Via the GPT-4o system prompt, which you configure during setup. ContentCore includes three pre-written brand voice variants (authoritative, conversational, and direct) in the README. You can also paste in examples of your existing content for the model to match.

Does it work with CMS platforms other than WordPress?

Yes. ContentCore uses the WordPress REST API by default. The README includes adaptation guides for Webflow (CMS API), Ghost (Admin API), and Contentful (Management API). Any CMS with a REST API can be wired in.

What does it cost to run?

Approximately $0.10–0.20 per article (OpenAI GPT-4o for writing) + $0.04 per image (DALL-E 3) + SerpAPI free tier covers 100 searches/month. At 10 articles/month, running cost is roughly $1.50–2.50/month.
