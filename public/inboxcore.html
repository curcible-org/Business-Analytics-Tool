FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before purchasing.

Does InboxCore reply to every email automatically?

Yes — except spam and newsletters, which are labelled and archived silently. All other categories receive a context-aware auto-reply. You can adjust which categories trigger replies in the workflow configuration.

Can I customise the reply tone and categories?

Yes. The GPT-4o prompt is fully editable — you define your business context, tone of voice, and category definitions. Three example prompt variants (formal, conversational, concise) are included in the README.

Will it ever send a wrong reply?

InboxCore includes an approval mode option in the README — it drafts the reply as a Gmail draft instead of sending it automatically, so you can review before sending. Most teams start in draft mode for 1–2 weeks before switching to full auto-reply.

What CRM does it support?

HubSpot out of the box. The workflow uses standard HTTP requests for CRM sync, so it can be adapted to Salesforce, Pipedrive, or any CRM with an API. Guidance for adapting to other CRMs is included in the README.

Is this a subscription?

No. InboxCore is a one-time purchase — $79. You own the workflow. Running costs are approximately $0.02–0.05 per email processed (OpenAI GPT-4o), paid directly to OpenAI at your usage level.
