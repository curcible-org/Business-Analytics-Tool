How it works

Import. Configure. Automate.

Each workflow is a ready-made n8n automation. You configure credentials, replace a handful of placeholders, and activate — typically under 2 hours.

01 · Purchase
Buy & download

Purchase on Gumroad. Download the zip containing the workflow JSON, README, and step-by-step credential guides.

02 · Import
Import into n8n

Open n8n → Settings → Import Workflow → select workflow.json. All nodes and connections load instantly.

03 · Configure
Set credentials

Follow CREDENTIALS.md to connect your APIs. Replace the YOUR_* placeholders. Most workflows are live in under 2 hours.

04 · Test
Run a test

Each workflow includes a test payload in README.md. Trigger it once to verify end-to-end execution.

05 · Activate
Go live

Flip the Active switch in n8n. Your automation runs continuously — on schedule, webhook, or both.

06 · Support
We're here

Stuck on a credential or node? Email info@curcible.com. We respond within 1 business day.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need coding experience?
No. n8n is a visual workflow builder. You import the JSON, configure credentials using the step-by-step guides included, replace a handful of placeholder values, and activate. Most users are live within 1–4 hours depending on the workflow.
Do I need an n8n account?
Yes. n8n is free to self-host (download from n8n.io) or available as a cloud service from $20/mo. All workflows are compatible with both. Self-hosting is free and gives you full control.
What are the ongoing API costs?
API costs are billed directly to your accounts (OpenAI, Twilio, etc.) — not through Curcible. Each product page lists estimated costs per execution. Most workflows cost $0.02–0.20 per run depending on the AI model and data volume.
Can I customise the workflows?
Yes, fully. You own the workflow JSON and can modify any node, prompt, or integration. The AI prompts in particular are designed to be customised — README.md in each package explains which nodes to adjust for your brand voice and ICP.
What if I get stuck during setup?
Email info@curcible.com with your order number. We respond within 1 business day. For faster resolution, include a screenshot of the error and the node name where you're stuck.
Is there a done-for-you option?
Yes. Curcible offers a DFY service where we configure, deploy, and maintain the workflow on your infrastructure. Setup fees start at $800 with flat monthly retainers from $250/mo. Email info@curcible.com to discuss.
